Our Journey

Company History

From a bold idea to a growing digital force — every milestone has shaped who we are today.

2022

The Idea is Born

IDEAS SOLUTION was conceived by a group of passionate IT students who saw a critical gap in affordable, quality digital services for small businesses and UMKMs in the Bekasi region.

2023

Official Launch

The company was officially founded, launching its first services including custom web development and IT maintenance. Our first clients were local UMKM businesses eager to go digital.

2024

Service Expansion

Extended our service portfolio to include software licensing (Windows & Microsoft Office), system development, and expanded our dedicated team with specialized roles in development, marketing, and project management.

2025 – Present

Scaling Towards Tomorrow

IDEAS SOLUTION continues to grow, onboarding enterprise clients and deepening its digital transformation impact across businesses and organizations throughout Indonesia.
